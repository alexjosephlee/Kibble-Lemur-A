Northwest Indiana Pet Project

General Information

What was orginally the Porter County Pet Project has now been re-named the Northwest Indiana Pet Project. We chose to re-name the program to expand our community outreach to those across NW Indiana. Currently the program is working to spay and neuter pets of approved clients from the pantry. The future of the Northwest Indiana Pet Project will rely on the support of the community. Check out our donate page for spay/neuter sponsorship! We hope that the Pet Project will grow to a variety of programs to help people in need!

• Low cost spay/neuter clinics
• Low cost vaccine clinics
• Emergency medical needs
• Fur Angel fund assisting families send their beloved pets to teh rainbow bridge when funds do not allow. An ill or aged pet should be able to go peacefully and not prolong suffering when funds do not allow for such services.
